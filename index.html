About


My name is Jordan Howard and I have always had an overwhelming passion for makeup. It is a medium that possesses a vast range of possibilities... endless even. I tend to lean more towards bright, impactful colors when completing a piece, and use blander nudes in my day to day. The love I possess for art and creativity inspired me to choose makeup as my subject for my LAUNCH Project. I hope you enjoy, and follow!

The Process

We began this process by creating a slide presentation explaining how we would achieve our goals. We began by conducting research and naming some influential people involved in our topic. My plan was to create a page and attempt to reach out to a popular makeup bag. Following that, I would research how, and attempt to, edit my photos of previous and current makeup looks and upload them. Thus, creating a visually appealing, accessible, and useful page to present my work.

Editing Content

To the right is an edited time lapse of how I used photoshop to color correct and clarify my photos. A little editing can make a badly lit, fuzzy, washed out photo turn into a saturated, focused masterpiece.

Interview

For my interview, I reached out to an artist who was not out of reach, yet just popular enough to be deemed slightly "Insta-famous". Bela, a 15 year old self taught makeup artist, has 21.5k followers and over 50 posts. When I reached out, she happily answered my questions. This helped be gain some insight into the possible future of my page, and how just one successful creator has acheived her following.

The Result

After interviews, editing, and uploading... I ended up with a functional Instagram page with a small following. I used hashtags with a large search rate to reach a bigger audience. I also implemented a usable business email for possible freelance work.

Content

My Instagram page contains close ups and wide shots of multiple beauty makeups I have done. I edited them using photoshop's camera raw filter and iris blur mode.
